Constant Competition

Weekly Seasonal Play

Fight for a spot in seasonal playoffs within your division in weakly matches. Place within the top part of your division to be invited to the playoffs

Playoffs

Represent your division in a large playoff bracket to decide which player or team is the best

Organized Tournaments

Compete for internet points! Tournaments are held on a regular basis to improve your standings on the seasonal ladders and provide alternative competitions.

Play Like the Pros

Participate in leagues similar to popular tournament formats such as Code S and Code A of the GSL for Starcraft 2, LCS for League of Legends, and best of 3 play for Hearthstone

Be Social

Organize practice matches with other teams, recruit free-agents onto your team and trade players
